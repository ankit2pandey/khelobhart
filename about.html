Welcome to Khelobhart!

Join our community and take your sports journey to the next level. Whether you’re a student looking for the perfect match, an employee managing teams and events, or an institute organizing sports tournaments, Khelobhart makes the process easy and seamless.

How to Register:

Use the form below to get started! Simply select your role—Student, Employee, or Institute—and fill out the required details. Once you’re registered, you’ll have access to everything Khelobhart has to offer, from ground bookings to match scheduling, event management, and more.

Why Register?


1. Students: Find and register for sports matches that match your interests and availability. Stay updated on upcoming events and track your participation history.

2. Employees: Manage teams, events, and participants efficiently. Whether you’re a coach, umpire, or event coordinator, we have the tools you need to streamline your work.

3. Institutes: Organize and manage sports events with ease. From booking venues to registering teams, Khelobhart is your all-in-one solution for sports event management.
